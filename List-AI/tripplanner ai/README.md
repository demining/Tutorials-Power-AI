## Tutorial: How to Use the TripPlanner AI Tool



### Step 1: Sign Up and Log In



- Visit the TripPlanner AI website or download the app.

- Create an account by providing your email address and setting a password.

- Log in to access the main dashboard.



### Step 2: Input Travel Details



- Click on the "Create New Trip" button.

- Enter your destination, travel dates, and the number of travelers.

- Specify your interests (e.g., adventure, relaxation, culture) to receive tailored recommendations.



### Step 3: Explore Recommendations



- Once your details are submitted, the AI will generate a list of suggested itineraries.

- Browse through the recommendations, which may include activities, restaurants, and accommodations.



### Step 4: Customize Your Itinerary



- Select activities and accommodations that appeal to you.

- Adjust the schedule by dragging and dropping items to fit your preferences.



### Step 5: Save and Share Your Itinerary



- After finalizing your plans, save the itinerary to your account.

- You can also share it with friends or family via email or social media.



### Step 6: Access Real-Time Updates



- During your trip, check the app for real-time updates on weather and local events.

- Make adjustments to your plans as needed based on the latest information.
