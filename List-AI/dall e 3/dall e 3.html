If required, create an account on the platform. This may involve providing an email

### Step 3: Navigating the Interface

Once logged in, familiarize yourself with the user interface. Key components typically

### Step 5: Generating Images

After entering your prompt, click the image generation button. DALL-E 3 will process your

request and display the generated images. You can review the results and choose to
