## Tutorial for Beginners

To begin using Searcholic, navigate to the official website or download the app from your

1. Click on the "Sign Up" button.

2. Fill in the required information, such as your email address and password.

3. Verify your email to activate your account.

### Step 3: Navigating the Interface

Upon logging in, you will be greeted with a user-friendly dashboard. Familiarize yourself

with the following sections:

- Search Bar: Located at the top, this is where you input your queries.

- Categories: On the side panel, you will find various categories to help narrow down your
