1. Select Your Video: Choose the video you want to dub. The tool typically supports

various video formats.

2. Upload the Video: Click on the upload button and select your video file from your

device.

### Step 3: Choosing Voice Settings

1. Select Language: Choose the language you want the dubbing to be in.

2. Pick a Voice: Browse through the available voice options. You can listen to samples to

3. Adjust Parameters: Modify the tone, pitch, and speed settings as needed.
