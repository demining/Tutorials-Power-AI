# How to Use and Navigate the AI: Be My Eyes Tool

## Introduction

In recent years, advancements in artificial intelligence (AI) have led to the development of

innovative tools designed to assist individuals with visual impairments. One such tool is

"Be My Eyes," an application that connects blind and low-vision users with sighted

volunteers through a live video call. This article explores how to use and navigate the Be

My Eyes tool, its benefits, classification, and provides a tutorial for beginners, including

references to helpful YouTube videos.

## Classification of Be My Eyes

Be My Eyes can be classified as an assistive technology tool that leverages AI and

crowdsourcing to provide real-time assistance. It falls under the following categories:

1. Assistive Technology: Tools designed to aid individuals with disabilities in performing

tasks that might otherwise be difficult or impossible.

2. Crowdsourced Support: The application connects users with a global network of

volunteers, allowing for immediate assistance.

3. AI Integration: While primarily a human-to-human connection tool, Be My Eyes is

integrating AI features to enhance user experience, such as providing automated

assistance for common tasks.

## Benefits of Be My Eyes

The Be My Eyes tool offers numerous benefits, including:

1. Immediate Assistance: Users can receive help in real-time, making it easier to navigate

daily tasks.

2. Community Support: The platform fosters a sense of community, connecting users with

volunteers who are eager to help.

3. Accessibility: The app is available on both iOS and Android devices, making it widely

accessible.

4. Diverse Use Cases: Users can seek assistance for various tasks, such as reading

labels, navigating unfamiliar environments, or identifying objects.

## Tutorial for Using Be My Eyes

### Step 1: Download and Install the App

- iOS Users: Visit the App Store, search for "Be My Eyes," and download the app.

- Android Users: Go to the Google Play Store, search for "Be My Eyes," and install the

app.

### Step 2: Create an Account

1. Open the app and select "Sign Up."

2. Enter your email address and create a password.

3. Confirm your account through the verification email sent to you.

### Step 3: Set Up Your Profile

- Provide information about your visual impairment to help volunteers understand your

needs better.

- You can also choose to receive assistance in your preferred language.

### Step 4: Request Assistance

1. Tap the "Call for Help" button.

2. The app will connect you to a volunteer via a live video call.

3. Explain your situation and what assistance you need.

### Step 5: End the Call

- Once your query is resolved, thank the volunteer and end the call.

### Step 6: Explore AI Features (if available)

- Be My Eyes is gradually integrating AI features that can assist with common tasks

without needing a volunteer. Keep an eye on updates for new functionalities.
